Earn Your Bachelors in Nursing, Advance Your Career

At GWU, we understand that nurses often feel called into their profession, and we work hard to make it easier for you to advance faster in this chosen field.

Fast Facts:

  • 100% online
  • No prerequisites
  • Designed for working nurses

Earn your Bachelor of Science in Nursing (BSN) online and you'll build your leadership skills, deepen your understanding of managing care for a large group of patients and provide a broader understanding of professional, ethical and legal standards, all of which will prepare you to advance your career. GWU will prepare you to practice nursing from a holistic, value-centered mindset and graduate ready to make a greater difference in the lives of your patients, and be a better communicator with patients, families and colleagues. This accelerated degree is focused on leadership skills, clinical competence, therapeutic relationships and social awareness while grounded in Christian values.

Designed with working nurses in mind, you can earn your BSN 100% online, and college credits you already have may be able to be applied to your degree. Most RNs entering the program complete their degrees in about a year. The program requires few prerequisites so you can finish your degree faster.
